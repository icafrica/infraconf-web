We've been building this quietly for a while. Today it's real.

**InfraConf Africa 2026** is Africa's first virtual conference dedicated *strictly* to infrastructure engineering — platform engineering, DevOps, SRE, cloud infrastructure, and the hard problems that come with building systems at scale on this continent.

## Why now?

African engineers have been showing up to KubeCon, SREcon, and PlatformCon for years — watching from recordings, buying $3,000 flights, or just missing out entirely. The infrastructure talent is here. The ambition is here. The community was waiting for a home.

Now it has one.

## What to expect

- **6 tracks** — platform engineering, cloud infra, reliability, Africa-specific infra, AI/ML infrastructure, and security
- **12–16 talks** from practitioners who've done the work
- **2 keynotes** to open and close the day
- **Free to attend** — no paywalls for the community

## Submit your talk

The CFP is now open. We want practitioners — people who have hit real walls and found real solutions. First-time speakers are actively encouraged and supported.

Deadline: **August 15, 2026**
