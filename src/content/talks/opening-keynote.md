## Abstract

This keynote opens InfraConf Africa 2026 with a single question: *why now?*

Africa has 700 million people under 30. It has the world's fastest-growing developer population. It has infrastructure challenges that are unlike anywhere else on earth — and solutions that the rest of the world has started to copy.

This talk traces the arc of African infrastructure engineering: from "we don't have the cloud region" to "we're building the cloud region." From "our engineers have to watch the replay" to "our engineers are on the stage."

We'll look at what's changed in the last five years — the new data centers, the regional cloud expansions, the fintech infrastructure that now processes billions of dollars daily, the open-source projects that started on the continent.

And we'll set the tone for the day: deep, practitioner-level content. No hype. No pitch decks. Just the actual work.
